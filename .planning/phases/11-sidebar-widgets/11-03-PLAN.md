**Data state:** Render a WidgetCard with title "Representing This Community" containing:
- Sort reps by `BRANCH_ORDER[rep.district_type]` (import from `src/types/representatives.ts`). Unknown district_types sort to 99.
- Filter out vacant seats (`is_vacant === true`)
- For each rep, render a compact card:
